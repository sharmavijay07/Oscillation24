Treasure Hunt

Rules

Participant Group Size:

Each participating group must consist of 3 to 4 members. This ensures effective collaboration and teamwork during the treasure hunt.

Solving Pseudo Codes for Clues:

Participants will be provided with pseudo codes that contain hints or instructions leading to the location of the next clue. Groups must work together to decipher the pseudo codes and locate the subsequent clues.

Clues Hidden Anywhere in the Campus:

Clues could be hidden anywhere within the PVPPCOE campus area. Participants must explore the campus, following the hints provided in the pseudo codes, to locate each clue.

Time Limit:

The treasure hunt will have a maximum duration of 3 hours. Within this time frame, groups must locate and solve as many clues as possible to progress through the treasure hunt.
